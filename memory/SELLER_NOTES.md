**ApnaGhr Platform**

## 1. Daily Reports (Mandatory)

### Morning - Daily Start Report
After every login, you must fill:
- Upload office selfie (optional)
- Today's plan
- Planned visits count
- Expected deals count

### Evening - Daily End Report
Before logout, you must fill:
- Clients called today
- Visits booked
- Deals closed
- Properties shared
- Tomorrow's planned visits

**Warning:** If Calls < 60 or Shares < 20 → Warning on next login

---

## 2. Client Verification (24-Hour Rule)

### What You Must Do:
After sharing a property with any client, you must update their status within 24 hours.

### How It Works:
1. You share property with client
2. After 24 hours, verification popup appears
3. You must verify before sharing more properties

### Verification Options:

| Select This | When |
|-------------|------|
| **Closed Won** | Deal done, client converted |
| **Closed Lost** | Client not interested |
| **Still in Progress** | Still talking, add notes |

### Important Rules:

**Share Lock:**
- Pending verifications older than 24 hours = Share button LOCKED
- Complete all verifications to unlock

**Account Lock:**
- More than 10 "Closed Lost" in 7 days = Account LOCKED
- Contact Admin immediately to unlock
