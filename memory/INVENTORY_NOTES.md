# Inventory Team Guide
**ApnaGhr Platform**

---

## 1. How to Login

1. Open ApnaGhr app
2. Login with your credentials (ask Admin)
3. Select **"Inventory Access"**
4. Enter the inventory key (ask Admin for key)
5. Select your name from the list
6. Take selfie (optional)
7. Select cities you will work on today
8. Set your target (default: 15 properties per city)
9. Click **"Start Session"**

---

## 2. Your Dashboard

After login, you will see:

| Item | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| **Added Today** | Properties you added today |
| **Target** | Your daily goal |
| **Points Earned** | 1 property = 1 point |
| **Progress Bar** | How close to target |

### Performance Status:

| Properties Added | Your Status |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Less than 30 | Needs Attention (Red) |
| 30 or more | Good Performance (Green) |

---

## 3. Adding Properties

1. Go to **"Inventory"** tab
2. Click **"Add Property"**
3. Fill all property details
4. Add photos
5. Save

Your count updates automatically!

---

## 4. What You Can Access

Only these tabs are available:
- My Dashboard
- Inventory (Add properties)
- Property Analytics

Other admin tabs are hidden.

---

## 5. End Your Session

When your work is done:
1. Go to your Dashboard
2. Click **"End Session"** button
3. Your work data is saved
4. Admin can see your performance

---

## 6. Daily Target

- Minimum target: **30 properties per day**
- Below 30 = "Needs Attention"
- 30 or above = "Good Performance"

---

## 7. Points System

| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Add 1 property | +1 point |

Your points are tracked daily and shown on dashboard.
